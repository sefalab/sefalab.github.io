Me in a nutshell

I am a computer science Master's student at Wits University in South Africa. My research interests include applying machine learning to help solve problems experienced by countries in the developing world. I enjoy working on computer vision problems and spatial data problems and a combination of both.

My master's work is about using satellite images and computer vision to study the effects and evolution of spatial apartheid in South Africa. I have also recently worked with the city of Jakarta, Indonesia through the data science for a social good fellowship to help improve traffic safety in Jakarta through traffic video analysis. In this project, we used computer vision and machine learning techniques to structure and then analyse video data.
